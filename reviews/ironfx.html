Background & Products/Assets:

IronFX is an international online trading broker, giving access to retail and institutional investors, to over 200 tradable instruments from 6 asset classes. IronFX is committed to provide the highest standard of services with full transparency to clients and become a one-stop destination for online trading. They provide state of the art order execution, competitive spreads, and most importantly our round-the-clock service and support.


Spreads on commonly traded instruments:

Ironfx offers 2 types of accounts: Micro Accounts and Premium Accounts.

Ironfx sets an accounts leverage based on the deposit amount. If exposure to a single currency is greater than 100 lots, leverage can be adjusted.

Platforms:

They offer two trading platforms which are both available on Apple, Android devices & Windows:

MetaTrader 4:

  • Mainly for Forex supporting stop, limit, market and trailing orders.

Personal Multi Account Manager (PMAM):

  • Using the sophisticated Personal Multi-Account Management (PMAM) platform, you are able to manage and trade on multiple MT4 accounts simultaneously.
  • Features include Orders, positions, equity and exposure online management.

WebTrader4:

  • WebTrader4 is an in house platform developed by ironFx.
  • They provide full access to multi-product trading accounts.

Demo:

A free demo account is available with no time limit with an initial virtual balance of $100,000.


Accounts:

Micro Accounts: Minimum deposit of $100 and Minimum trade size of 0.01 lot.

Premium: Minimum deposit of $1000 and Minimum trade size of 0.1 lot.

Ironfx are contactable through live chat on their website, email and the phone.


Deposits:
