Background & Products/Assets:

Etoro is a social trading and multi asset brokerage company. They focus on financial and copy trading services. Starting out in 2007 they have grown their business into a global company with offices located in Cyprus, Isreal, United Kingdom, United States and Australia.

Regulation:

Etoro is authorised and regulated by the Cyprus Securities Exchange Commission (CySEC), the UK’s Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and the European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA).


Platforms:

Etoro’s platform is called Etoro Copy trader. Their platform is considered among the leaders of the fintech revolution. The tool also delivers excellent technical analysis and research abilities. It is user friendly and well designed which is available on desktop and mobile. Unfortunately Etoro does not offer MT4 to their traders, as their Etoro platform is not compatible with MT4 so their social investors would not be able to benefit.


Accounts:

Etoro offers two types of accounts which include retail accounts and professional accounts. Retail accounts – They will be able to receive access to all the trading assets, and will be able to trade both manually or via Copy Trade. While the leverage is restricted, they will be able to receive negative balance protection and margin closeout restrictions. Professional accounts – These clients have successfully passed the test to determine their suitability to qualify as a professional client. These clients will be given negative balance protection and they will receive flexible leverage up to 1:400 for certain instruments, and will also receive a reduction in the current margin rates.


Supports:

Etoro offer support Monday to Friday 24 hours a day. Contact can be made via email, phone or by using the web contact form provided on their website.


Withdrawals:

This can be done via Debit and Credit cards. For the traders who do not possess this they are able to fund their account via bank wire transfer. The minimum withdrawal amount allowed is $50 dollars which may take up to 3 working days. Etoro also charge a fee of $5 for every withdrawal made.


Countries not accepted:

Etoro does not accept traders from Canada, Turkey, Japan or Iran.
