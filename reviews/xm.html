Background & Products/Assets:

Part of XM Group which is part of Trading Point of Financial Instruments UK Limited. 1.5 million clients in 196 countries. XM broker platforms support the trading of forex, stocks, commodities, equity indices, precious metals, energies and cryptocurrencies.


Spreads on commonly traded instruments:

Depends on the type of account opened as XM offer 3 types of accounts: Micro Accounts, Standard Accounts and XM Zero Accounts.

Minimum spread across all is 0.1 pips, the average spread for a major pair E.g. EURUSD is 0.1 pips.

Commission is only given for XM Zero Accounts.

Leverage

Depending on the instrument, leverage can range from 2:1 to 30:1. Clients offered the chance to manage their own leverage risk. Margin requirements remain constant throughout the week. Traders in Non-European markets will be able to access greater levels of leverage and trade with certain EEA restrictions removed.


Regulation:

XM is regulated by the following:

  • XM.com and the parent company, XM Group, is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.

Platforms:

They offer two trading platforms which are both available on Apple and Android devices:

MetaTrader 4:

  • Mainly for Forex supporting stop, limit, market and trailing orders.

MetaTrader 5:

  • MetaTrader 5: offering access to stocks, stock indices and precious metals.

Accounts:

  • Micro Accounts: Micro accounts can use USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, AUD, HUF and PLN as the base currency and can get started with a minimum deposit of $5USD. 1 micro lot is 1,000 units of the base currency.
  • Standard: Standard accounts can use USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, AUD, HUF and PLN as the base currency and traders can get started with just $5USD. 1 standard lot is 100,000 units of the chosen base currency.
  • Zero Accounts: Zero accounts can use USD and EUR as the base currency and traders will require a minimum deposit of $10USD. Like the Standard account, 1 standard lot is 100,000 units of the chosen base currency.

Benefits:

  • Educational material is provided to those beginning as traders.
  • Expert analysis and tracking tools are provided for more experienced traders.
  • XM also publish research and technical analysis.

Support:

XM are contactable through live chat on their website, email and the phone. A VPS (virtual private server) is provided to help increase the speed of trades. It is accessible from anywhere and is also available 24/7. XM also offer commission and fee free withdrawals and deposits.


Withdrawals:

XM covers all transfer fees and same day withdrawals are guaranteed.


Accepted Countries:

Australia, Thailand, United Kingdom, South Africa, Singapore, Hong Kong, India, France, Germany, Norway, Sweden, Italy, Denmark, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Luxembourg, Qatar and most other countries.


Countries not accepted:

United States, Canada, Israel, Iran, Belgium.
