Background & Products/Assets:

FxPro UK Limited is a leading online broker offering Contracts for Difference (CFDs) and Spread Betting on forex, futures, spot indices, shares, spot metals and spot energies.

FxPro serves clients in over 170 countries worldwide, have been in the industry for over 15 years, have received over 60 international awards and were the first broker to sponsor an F1 team.


Spreads on commonly traded instruments:

Spreads dependent on the type of account. MT4 minimum fixed spread quotes are roughly 1.2pips. With an MT5 account, minimum live spreads are 1.4pips. With a FxPro cTrader account, minimum live spreads are 0.3pips. FxPro only provide fix spreads for 7 main currency pairs, and prices may still fluctuate due to market conditions.

Regulation:

  • FxPro Financial Services Limited is authorised by the Financial Sector Conduct Authority ('FSCA').

Platforms:

They offer two trading platforms which are both available on Apple and Android devices:

MetaTrader 4:

  • Mainly for Forex supporting stop, limit, market and trailing orders.
  • Also gives access to Metals, Futures, Shares, Indices and Cryptocurrencies.

MetaTrader 5:

  • Offering access to Forex & Metals, Futures, Indices and Cryptocurrencies.

FxPro Edge:

  • Highly customisable platform
  • Enables traders to place orders via the web, no need to download or install software

FxPro cTrader:

  • Powerful platform offering best available bid and ask prices with orders filled in milliseconds.

Accounts:

  • For Fixed Spread accounts, MT4 accounts may work well.
  • FxPro does not offer ECN or micro accounts.
  • Demo accounts available on the MT4, MT5 and cTrader platforms..
  • Demo accounts provide a virtual $500,000 to work with.

Additional Features:

FxPro provide a range of education, news and analytics services to help traders with their pre and post trade analysis. FxPro also provide calculators that traders can use.


Deposits and withdrawals

FxPro Wallet, Debit, credit and prepaid cards, eWallets and a series of other payment methods depending on your location. FxPro makes funding and withdrawing funds even easier as FxPro Wallet allows you to transfer funds between your FxPro wallet and trading account at any time you wish.

Withdrawal times are kept to 1 business day. There is no charge on bank wire transactions for withdrawing revenue generated. However, the sending and correspondent bank may charge according to their own fee structure.


Support:

  • Support available in multiple languages.
  • Traders can contact FxPro via email, live chat or the phone.
  • Wait times are relatively low and FxPro provide a detailed FAQ page.
