Regulation:

HYCM is regulated by the following:

  • HYCM is authorised and regulated by the UK’s Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)
  • HYCM is authorised and regulated by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC)

Platforms:

The three main platforms offered by HYCM are MT4, MT5 and its in-house web-based platform PrimeTrader.

PrimeTrader:

  • Provides integrated multiple pricing
  • Advance charts and online account management.

MT4 and MT5:

  • Ability to trade using a mobile app and access technical analysis on live charts. There are three types of account available.

Demo Accounts:

HYCM provide a demo account where you have $50,000 in virtual money to trade with. The demo account can be used for as long as you wish and can be funded at any time.


Accounts:

  • Fixed: fixed spreads from 1.8pips, no commission, $100 min deposit and min trade volume of 0.01.
  • Classic: variable spreads from 1.2pips, no commission, $100 min deposit, EA available and min trade volume of 0.01.
  • Raw: raw spreads from 0.2pips +$4 per round, $200 min deposit, EA available and min trade volume of 0.01.
  • HYCM also offer ‘VIP Accounts’ with a dedicated account manager, comprehensive market analytics and better spreads.

Additional Features

HYCM offer a range of additional features to aid traders such as calculators, an economic calendar, news and a market outlook. They also offer educational resources such as a Forex knowledge base, webinars, workshops and seminars.


Offers:

HYCM offer a 10% deposit bonus if certain conditions are met (such as minimum deposit for a new account)


Supports:

Support is available 24/5 via phone, email and live chat.


Deposit methods:

Clients can make withdrawals and deposits using credit and debit cards (Visa and Mastercard), wire transfer, Skrill, Neteller and WebMoney. The two base currencies that accounts are offered in are USD and EUR. Deposits are also accepted in KWD, SAR, AED and are converted to USD for trading. HYCM do not charge deposit fees. The minimum deposit/withdrawal is $20, apart for wire transfers where the amount is $250 for deposits and £300 for withdrawals.
