Background & Products/Assets:

Plus500 are the Leading CFD trading platform. They are owned by a company called Plus500 Ltd and are listed on main market of the London Stock Exchange (LON:PLUS). Plus500 are known around the globe as they are regulated around the world and are an active sponsor of professional Soccer and Rugby teams and also an official sponsor of Club Atletico de Madrid since 2016.

The instruments Plus500 offer are CFD's on Indices, Forex, Commodities, Crypto, Shares, Options and ETFs and 70 CFD Forex currency pairs. The Leverage can go up to 1:30 (higher for professional clients), although Cryptocurrency leverage is capped at 1:2. PLus500 offer clients more than 2000 instruments overall to trade on so their is a wide range.

Reputation:

Since its inception in 2008, it has developed a reputation of being one of the most trusted brokers. Its reputation is aided by the fact that it is listed on the Main Market of the London Stock Exchange.


Regulation:

Plus500 is regulated by the following:

  • Plus500UK Ltd authorized & regulated by the FCA (#509909).

Platforms:

Web Trader:

  • Plus500's proprietary trading platform
  • Users can download and install on their computer.
  • Both Retail and Professional accounts operate from the same platform.
  • Users can also use the web-based version on a web browser.

Mobile Trading:

A free mobile trading app available through the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store. The mobile app provides all of the options that the web-based version does.


Accounts:

Plus500 offer free unlimited demo accounts.

There is only one type of live account which is a Standard Trading Account. However, clients classed as professional have leverage increased to 1:300 and have fewer restrictions on margins. $100 minimum deposit is required for a live account. The Inactivity fee on a live account is up to 10 USD.


Offers:

Due to recent changes in the regulatory environment, all sign up and deposit promotions have ended.


Support:

Email and chat support available 24/7. Website available in 32 languages, but no telephone support is offered.


Withdrawals:

  • Payment and withdrawal methods: Credit/Debit cards (Visa or Mastercard), Paypal, Skrill (Moneybookers) or Bank Transfer.
  • Minimum deposit: $100
  • Withdrawals have a processing time of 3 business days. Withdrawal methods have a minimum amounts threshold.
  • You can only withdraw using the method used to initially make your deposit.
  • No withdrawal fee.
