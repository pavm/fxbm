How To Choose Your Broker

Choosing a broker is one of the biggest steps in the process of beginning to trade. The broker will be responisble for holding your money and it is the broker that you will work through to open postions on market.
There are a number of things that one should look into when selecting a broker and below we will go over the most important.

Trading Platforms

All of your trades will be processed through a trading platform, before signing up with a trader make sure to check which trading platforms they support.


Compatibility of these platforms is one of the most important features that should be investgiated which devices will the trading platform funciton on?


User Interface and Functionality. Is the trading platform easy to use? What indicators does the platform include? Is it what you require to perform technical analysis?


Desposits and Withdrawal

The most important attribute a broker should have is, a good broker will make it a priortiy to provide an easy and smooth process of depositing and withdrawing funds.


Humans are usually attracted to procceses that are quick and easy, specially if it is to do with their funds. If the broker provides this service at the best they can without any hassle, they will attract the most people.


Security

One of the top priorities brokers hold is to provide their clients security, only then would they win the trust of people to handle their funds.


The easiest way to figure out if a broker is secure is to do some research into which regulatories the broker follows and which one applies to where you are in the world.


The few main authories would be:


  • The FCA - The Financial Conduct Authority (United Kingdom)
  • The CTFC - The Comodities Future Trading Commission (United States of America)
  • The NFA - The National Futures Association (United States of America)
  • The AMF - Autorité des Marchés Financiers (France)
  • The SFBC - The Swiss Federal Banking Commission

The list continues but im sure by now, looking for these securities in brokers is a mandatory understanding.


Speed

Speeds are very important when choosing a broker, when opening a postioning you need guranteed your postion is opened within Microlots of where you intended. This ensures that limits youve set are met and postions are opened at times and locations to where you intend them to be.


If trades arent opened in a decent amount of time, traders could loose distance currencies travel, pips could be missed which then further impacts how much profit is made.
